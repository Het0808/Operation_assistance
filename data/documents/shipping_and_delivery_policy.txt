CATEGORY: Policy
LAST UPDATED: 2025-10-15
OWNER: Operations Manager

---

SCOPE
Covers all outbound shipments from the main warehouse to domestic customers.
International shipping is handled by a separate third-party arrangement and is out of scope here.

STANDARD SHIPPING
- Processing time: 1–2 business days after order confirmation.
- Delivery window: 5–7 business days after dispatch.
- Carrier: FastFreight Logistics (primary), BackupCarrier Co. (overflow).
- Tracking numbers are emailed automatically within 24 hours of dispatch.

EXPEDITED SHIPPING
- Available for an additional $12.99 per order.
- Processing time: same day if order placed before 13:00 local warehouse time.
- Delivery window: 2–3 business days after dispatch.
- Not available for oversized items (any dimension > 80 cm or weight > 25 kg).

OVERSIZED ITEMS
Items flagged OVERSIZED in the product catalog ship via freight only.
Freight delivery windows: 7–14 business days.
A delivery appointment must be scheduled; the warehouse will contact the customer within 2 business days
of dispatch to arrange a time slot.

FREE SHIPPING THRESHOLD
Orders with a subtotal of $75 or more qualify for free standard shipping.
The threshold applies after any discounts are applied but before taxes.

ADDRESS CHANGES
Address changes are accepted up to 1 hour after order confirmation.
After that window, the order is locked for dispatch and cannot be rerouted.
Customers must contact support@opsco.internal immediately if an address error is made.

FAILED DELIVERIES
If a delivery attempt fails twice, the package is held at the carrier depot for 5 business days.
If unclaimed, it is returned to the warehouse and the order is flagged for refund processing
minus a $8.50 return-freight fee.
