OVERVIEW
This document covers handling, storage, and known issues for the electronics product range stocked
in the main warehouse. SKU prefix for all electronics: EL-XXXXX.

STORAGE REQUIREMENTS
- Temperature: 10°C to 30°C. Do not store near loading bay doors in winter.
- Humidity: 30%–70% RH. Electronics shelf (Zone C) is climate-monitored.
- Keep away from magnetic equipment and heavy machinery.

HIGH-VELOCITY PRODUCTS (current top sellers)
1. EL-10042 — Wireless Charging Pad (15W)
   - Reorder point: 20 units. Supplier B lead time: 12 business days.
   - Known issue: units manufactured before batch B24-07 may not charge cases thicker than 4 mm.
     Affected units have a serial number starting with WCP-2023. Replace on request, no questions asked.

2. EL-10087 — Bluetooth Noise-Cancelling Headphones
   - Reorder point: 15 units. Supplier B lead time: 14 business days.
   - Firmware v1.2 (shipped from Oct 2025 onward) fixes random disconnect bug in v1.1.
     Customers with v1.1 headphones should be directed to the firmware update page.

3. EL-10115 — USB-C 65W Laptop Charger (universal)
   - Reorder point: 25 units. Supplier A lead time: 6 business days.
   - Compatible with most laptops; not compatible with Apple MagSafe-only models.
     Product listing updated Oct 2025 to clarify compatibility; returns from before that date are accepted.

OVERSIZED FLAG
No current electronics items are flagged OVERSIZED. Items above 2 kg are noted in the catalog
but do not exceed the freight dimension threshold.

WARRANTY NOTES
All electronics carry a 12-month manufacturer warranty.
Warranty claims go directly through the manufacturer; support provides the manufacturer contact
and RMA reference if requested. Do not process manufacturer warranty claims through the returns flow.
