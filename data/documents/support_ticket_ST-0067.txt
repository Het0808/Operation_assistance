external impact (inner foam shows compression mark matching crack location).
Outer box shows no damage — carrier likely not at fault.
Policy (product_damage_claims_procedure.txt) requires claims within 72 hours; customer is outside
the window. However, damage appears to be a packaging/manufacturing defect, not customer-caused.

AGENT ASSESSMENT
Standard policy would deny the claim (outside 72-hour window).
However, the nature of the damage (hidden under foam, not immediately visible) and the photo
evidence suggest this is a legitimate manufacturing defect.
Agent is not authorised to approve a replacement outside the 72-hour window without escalation.

ESCALATION TO OPERATIONS MANAGER
Ticket escalated on 2025-11-18. Awaiting decision.
Customer notified of escalation and estimated 2-business-day response time.
