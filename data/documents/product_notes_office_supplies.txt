OVERVIEW
Covers the office supplies range. SKU prefix: OF-XXXXX.
This range is high-volume, low-margin. Fulfilment speed and accuracy are the priority.

STORAGE REQUIREMENTS
- Ambient temperature and humidity acceptable for all items.
- Paper products (OF-20010 to OF-20099): keep away from damp areas; pallets must be off the floor.
- Ink cartridges (OF-20200 series): do not freeze; shelf life 18 months from manufacture date.
  Rotate stock using FIFO. Expired cartridges must be quarantined and returned to Supplier A.

HIGH-VELOCITY PRODUCTS
1. OF-20010 — A4 Copy Paper, 80gsm (500 sheets/ream)
   - Sold in cases of 5 reams. Reorder point: 50 cases.
   - No known issues. Standard item; qualifies for free shipping threshold calculation.

2. OF-20045 — Ballpoint Pen Multipack (10-pack, blue/black/red)
   - Reorder point: 80 units. Fast mover — check stock daily.
   - Supplier A has occasional lead time extension to 8 days in Q4 due to demand surge.
     Place early reorders in October to buffer against this.

3. OF-20201 — Black Ink Cartridge (compatible, 3-pack)
   - Reorder point: 30 units.
   - IMPORTANT: Compatibility list covers HP, Canon, and Epson models only (see compatibility sheet
     attached to product listing). Returns accepted if customer purchased an incompatible model
     and the cartridge is unopened.

4. OF-20310 — Ergonomic Desk Organiser
   - Reorder point: 15 units.
   - Comes flat-packed. Assembly instructions are in the box. No tool required.
   - Minor cosmetic variation between batches (colour shade) is expected and not a defect.

BUNDLE NOTE
OF-20010 + OF-20201 are frequently purchased together. A pre-bundled SKU (OF-99001) is available
at a $2 discount. Inventory is tracked separately per component; the bundle is assembled on pick.

FINAL_SALE ITEMS
OF-20400 series (seasonal planners, prior year) are marked FINAL_SALE. No returns.
