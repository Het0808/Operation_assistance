DOCUMENT: Returns and Refunds Policy
CATEGORY: Policy
LAST UPDATED: 2025-11-01
OWNER: Operations Manager

---

SCOPE
This policy applies to all customer orders fulfilled by the warehouse. It covers physical goods only.
Digital licenses and consumable items (opened packaging) are excluded.

RETURN WINDOW
- Standard items: 30 days from confirmed delivery date.
- Electronics and high-value items (unit price > $500): 15 days from confirmed delivery date.
- Clearance or final-sale items: No returns accepted. Items are marked FINAL_SALE in the product catalog.

CONDITION REQUIREMENTS
Items must be returned in original packaging with all accessories included.
Items showing signs of physical damage caused by the customer, misuse, or tampering are not eligible.
Missing accessories will result in a partial refund. Deduction schedule:
  - Missing power adapter: deduct $15
  - Missing user manual: deduct $5
  - Missing original box: deduct $10

REFUND PROCESSING
Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 7 business days of the warehouse receiving
and inspecting the returned item.
If the original payment method is no longer active, a store credit voucher is issued instead.

EXCEPTIONS
If an item arrives damaged or defective, the customer may request a replacement or full refund
within 72 hours of delivery by contacting support (support@opsco.internal) with photo evidence.
In such cases, the customer does not need to return the item unless specifically asked.

RESTOCKING FEE
A 10% restocking fee applies to orders over $200 that are returned for reasons other than
defect or damage. This fee is waived for customers with Gold or Platinum account tier.
